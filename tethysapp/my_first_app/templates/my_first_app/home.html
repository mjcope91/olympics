Welcome to the Olympic Games App!

App Overview

The Olympic Games App can be used to view the most recent 16 locations of the summer and winter Olympic Games. Each of the 16 Olympic Game locations can be viewed on an interactive map. As different locations are selected, the user is presented with a popup containing information about the Olympic Games at that location. The information includes the city, country, season, and year of the Olympic Games along with a photo and link to the Olympic Game website for the specific location area also provided.The different locations can be selected by clicking on them directly, or selecting them from the drop down menu. The 2016 Olympic torch path can also be viewed.
